About Symptom Solver

Symptom Solver is an innovative platform designed to revolutionize the way individuals approach healthcare. Our project aims to leverage the power of Machine Learning to analyze symptoms, providing fast and accurate disease predictions. This tool is an essential step towards making healthcare more accessible and efficient, helping users make informed decisions. By integrating advanced algorithms and user-friendly interfaces, we bridge the gap between technology and health, ensuring better outcomes for everyone.

In addition to predicting diseases, Symptom Solver also suggests some appropriate medications based on the predicted condition. For each disease, we provide detailed information including the medicine name, dosage, duration, and potential side effects. This feature is designed to help users make informed choices regarding their healthcare, ensuring they have the right medication to address their symptoms safely and effectively.
